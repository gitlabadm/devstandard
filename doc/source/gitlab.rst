GitLab Tutorial
===============

GitLab is a open sourced software, and it has two versions: community version and enterprise version.
`gitlab.com <https://gitlab.com>`_ is hosted by gitlab.org, and everyone can put their code on it. Or you can
deploy gitlab in your own machine.
